My Training Philosophy

Consistency Over Perfection

I focus on repeating fundamentals daily rather than chasing instant mastery. Small improvements compound over time.

Dedicated Practice

Every training session is structured to target specific weaknesses and build strength in key areas of the game.

Mental Toughness

Cricket is as much a mental game as it is physical. I work on developing resilience and staying focused under pressure.
